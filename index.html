SlyContacts

Contacts replacement for iPhone.

SlyContacts is your ultimate personal assistant when it comes to organizing and differentiating your contacts. Over time your phonebook can get pretty crowded, and it can be very difficult to keep track of all the people that you've added, particularly if the contact entry is missing a last name or some other distinguishing note.


SlyContacts solves this problem by providing you with a variety of tools to help keep all your contacts straight.

Download on the App Store

Chronological Contacts

SlyContacts will organize all your contacts by chronological order so you can see exactly when you added them to your phone.

Sneaky Photos

SlyContacts will use the FaceTime camera to save several behind the scenes photos of a person as they enter their information into your phone.

GPS Organization

Coming Soon SlyContacts will soon be adding GPS capability so that you can also know exactly where you were when a new contact was added.

Dialer Input

Coming Soon Ability to input contacts in a 'dialer' look a like screen.
