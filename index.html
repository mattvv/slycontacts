Sly Contacts

A simple contact replacement for iPhone

SlyContacts

SlyContacts is a new way of entering contacts. Inspired by my friend TK whome seems to forget a lot after a big night out, he frequently ended up with contacts in his phone whome he did not know of?!

SlyContacts solves this problem - firstly, contacts are sorted by date - and secondly, SlyContacts captures photos of the time and place when the contact was entered to help poor TK remember next morning after a big one!

We're waiting to be approved by apple - It all rests on this!
